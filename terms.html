WHAT IF MULTIVERSE — COMMUNITY RULES (DMO‑SAFE VERSION)

1. Content Ownership

Writers keep full ownership of the chapters they create.

By posting on this site, writers give the platform and other users a DMO license:

  • Derivative
  • Modified
  • Original

This means other users may:

  • Create branches
  • Continue your chapter
  • Rewrite alternate versions
  • Remix or expand your ideas

You still own your original chapter.

2. DMO License (Core Rule)

By posting a chapter, you agree to:

“Anyone may create derivative, modified, or original works based on this chapter, including branching stories, alternate timelines, and remixes.”

This license is:

  • Irrevocable (you can’t take it back later)
  • Non‑exclusive (you still own your work)
  • Royalty‑free (no payments required)
  • Worldwide

3. Attribution

  • You must credit the original chapter author
  • The platform automatically shows the branch tree
  • You may not claim someone else’s work as your own

4. Allowed Content

This platform is SFW‑only. Allowed content includes:

  • Fantasy
  • Sci‑fi
  • Adventure
  • Slice of life
  • Comedy
  • Light romance
  • Action
  • Worldbuilding
  • Cultivation
  • Multiverse timelines

5. Not Allowed (Hard Bans)

These rules keep the platform legally safe:

  • No NSFW or explicit sexual content
  • No minors in sexual situations
  • No graphic gore
  • No hate speech
  • No harassment
  • No doxxing
  • No real‑world extremist content
  • No copyrighted material copied from books, movies, games, etc.
  • No AI‑generated content that violates copyright

6. User Responsibilities

  • Only upload content you created
  • No plagiarism
  • No impersonation
  • No bypassing moderation
  • No harmful files (scripts, malware, etc.)

7. Moderation

The platform may:

  • Remove content that breaks rules
  • Lock or delete stories
  • Suspend accounts
  • Remove illegal or harmful content without warning

Moderation decisions are final.

8. Reporting

Users can report:

  • Rule violations
  • Plagiarism
  • Harassment
  • Illegal content

9. Liability

  • Users are responsible for what they post
  • The platform is not liable for user‑generated content
  • The platform may cooperate with legal authorities if required

10. Branching & Multiverse Rules

To keep the multiverse clean and fun:

  • Branch respectfully
  • Don’t sabotage someone’s story
  • Don’t overwrite or delete another user’s chapter
  • Alternate timelines must follow site rules
  • You may create “What if…” branches, AU versions, rewrites, side stories, and divergent paths

Short Version

By posting on What If Multiverse, you agree to the DMO license:

  • Your chapters can be branched, remixed, and continued by other users.
  • You keep ownership, but you allow derivative works.
  • All content must be SFW. No hate, no harassment, no illegal content.
